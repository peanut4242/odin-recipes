Noodles

Description

Want a hearty dose of delicous buttered noodles? This quick and easy recipe is sure to fulfill your needs!

Ingredients

  • 2,5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • .5 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients.
  2. Stir flour and salt together in large bowl. Add eggs, milk and butter
  3. Knead dough until smooth. Rest in bowl for 10 mins
  4. Roll out dough on floured surface
  5. Air dry for 15 mins
  6. Cook in large pot for 5 mins
  7. Serve and enjoy
